Frequently Asked Questions

Get help

You can get the information about ITS tests from any of the following sources: ITS website and advertisements through Print Media.

You can get Application Forms from ITS website i.e., http://www.itspak.com.pk. However, ensure that you get these before the last date for registration.

Fill your application form according to the given instructions . Submit it by paying the online Test Fee in any branch of UBL or HBL.

After submitting the Application Form you will get your Roll Number Slip (Call Letter) containing Roll No., Test Venue, etc, on your ITS website. This slip confirms your registration. You must take that slip with you to the test center on the day of your test otherwise you are not allowed to sit in the Examination Hall. You may also take the print of your information from ITS website.

In case of any confusion or problems with the registration process you can Contact ITS Office.

The registration of candidates on the day of ITS Examination cannot be entertained. The candidate should get registered before the deadline, announced by ITS for the close of registration process.
