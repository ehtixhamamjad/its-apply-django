How to Apply

Instructions to perform various operations on ITS portal

  • Login Instructions
  • Registration Instructions
  • Forget Password Instructions
  • If you are already registered on ITS Candidate Portal, then click on enter your CNIC Number and 6-digits password to login to your candidate portal
  • If you are not registered on ITS Candidate Portal, click on 'Create an Account' link below to create a new account
  • If you are registered on ITS Candidate Portal, but you have forgotten your password, then click on 'Forget Password' link to recover your password.
  • Enter your Full Name as per your CNIC
  • Enter your CNIC number
  • If your CNIC or mobile number is already registered on the portal then go to login page to login to your account
  • Enter your email address. This field is optional. If you don't have an email address then leave this field empty
  • Click on 'Send Verification Code' button to send a verification code / OTP on your entered mobile number
  • After a few seconds, you will receive a four-digit verification code / OTP
  • If you dont receive the verification code / OTP, then you can resend the code after 1 minute
  • After receiving the Verification Code / OTP, enter the code below and click on 'Sign Up' button
  • After a few seconds, you will receive a 6-digits password on your mobile number through which you can login to your account
  • Enter your CNIC number
  • Enter your mobile number
  • Click on 'SEND VERIFICATION CODE' button
  • If your given CNIC and mobile number is not linked with any account, the system will show you a message!
  • Enter your email address. This field is optional. If you don't have an email address then leave this field empty
  • Click on 'Send Verification Code' button to send a verification code / OTP on your entered mobile number
  • After a few seconds, you will receive a four-digit OTP / Verification Code on your mobile number
  • Enter the received OTP / Verification Code in the below field and click on 'SEND NEW PASSWORD' button
  • After a few seconds, you will receive a new password on your mobile number through which you can login to your account
